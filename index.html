Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Disney+?
    Disney+ is the streaming home of Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, National Geographic, and Star. It is the exclusive streaming home for new movies from Disney, Pixar, and Marvel, as well as a vast collection of classic movies and TV series from these brands. With Disney+, you can enjoy unlimited access to a wide range of content for a monthly or annual subscription fee.

      • What is Star+?
        Star+ is a streaming service that offers a wide range of content, including movies, TV shows, and original programming. It is a separate service from Disney+ and is available in select regions. Star+ features content from various studios and networks, providing a diverse selection of entertainment options for subscribers.
        • How much does Disney+ cost?
          Disney+ offers different subscription plans depending on the region. In many countries, there are monthly and annual subscription options available. The pricing may vary, so it's best to check the official Disney+ website for the most up-to-date information on subscription costs in your specific location.
          • Devices you can watch on
            You can watch Disney+ on a variety of devices, including smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, streaming devices (such as Roku and Amazon Fire TV), gaming consoles (like PlayStation and Xbox), and web browsers on computers. The service is designed to be accessible across multiple platforms, allowing you to enjoy your favorite content wherever you prefer.
            • When can I cancel my subscription?
              You can cancel your Disney+ subscription at any time. If you cancel, you will still have access to the service until the end of your current billing period. After that, your subscription will not renew, and you will lose access to the content. It's important to note that cancellation policies may vary depending on the platform or payment method you used to subscribe.
